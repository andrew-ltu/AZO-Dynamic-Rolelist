Field Notes

About AZO

AZO is an ArmA 3 milsim unit built around structured gameplay, professional leadership, and a focus on community. Our operations combine infantry manoeuvres, armour support, rotary-wing CAS, and medical logistics across dynamic mission sets.

⚔️

Combined Arms

Every operation integrates infantry, armour, aviation, and support elements for a fully realised combined-arms experience.

📋

Structured Roster

Role-based sign-ups with endorsement tracking for medical, JTAC, and leadership positions. Know your slot before you deploy.

🎖️

Promotion System

Track your progression from Recruit through to SOCOMD. Promotions are based on attendance, aptitude, and leadership potential.

🤝

Community Focus

Everyone has a voice. Feedback drives how we shape operations, SOPs, and the unit's direction. New ideas are always welcome.
