⚙️ Admin SOPs

Administrative and leadership procedures

1. Zeus Responsibilities

1.1 Pre-Operation Planning

  • Create mission briefing and objectives
  • Design balanced scenarios for player count
  • Test mission for technical issues
  • Coordinate with team leaders on force composition
  • Prepare backup plans for contingencies

1.2 Operation Execution

  • Conduct pre-mission briefing clearly and concisely
  • Monitor mission pacing and player engagement
  • Adapt difficulty based on player performance
  • Provide tactical challenges without unfair situations
  • Maintain immersion and narrative coherence

2. Team Leader Duties

2.1 Personnel Management

  • Track team member attendance
  • Assign roles based on qualifications and experience
  • Mentor new members
  • Provide feedback and performance reviews

2.2 Tactical Leadership

  • Brief team on specific objectives
  • Coordinate with other team leaders
  • Make tactical decisions within Zeus guidance
  • Ensure proper communications protocol

3. Member Management

3.1 Role Assignments

  • Review member certifications in members.json
  • Assign roles through the roster system
  • Update member endorsements as earned
  • Track operational experience (opsAttended)

3.2 Discord OAuth & Database

  • Monitor user authentication logs
  • Grant/revoke admin privileges in database
  • Assign team roles (user_roles table)
  • Review SOP access logs for security
